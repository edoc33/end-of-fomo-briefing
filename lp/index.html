Lineage · five named moments, 23 years

The category formed in plain sight.

A timeline of when the work of watching what changes became visible, and what each step left unnamed.

May 2003

Google Alerts

The largest crawler on earth tells you when something new appears. Free, integrated into Search, keyword-driven.

The category exists. It has no consumer name yet.

google.com / alerts
2017

Visualping

Per-page visual diffing opens to the public. JavaScript-rendered pages become first-class citizens of the monitoring stack.

The precision layer ships. Still no consumer name.

visualping.io
September 2025

ChatGPT Pulse

OpenAI ships daily proactive briefings for Pro subscribers at $200 a month. A consumer AI feature whose job is to deliver information without being asked.

The behavior gets traction. The category still has no name.

openai.com / pulse
May 2026

Exa raises $250M

A $2.2 billion valuation, led by Andreessen Horowitz. Developer API for recurring web searches at the open-web layer.

Capital validates the layer. Still no consumer-named category.

exa.ai / series-c
Summer 2026

Google Information Agents

I/O 2026: Google ships persistent AI agents inside Search, rolling out to AI Pro and Ultra subscribers at $20 to $200 per month.

23 years in, the category gets a consumer name.

blog.google / I/O 2026
